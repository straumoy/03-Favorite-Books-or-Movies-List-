My Top 5 Favorite Movies

I love watching movies! Here are five of my all-time favorites (in no real order):

  • The Matrix - How can you work with IT and not like this movie?
  • Jurrasic Park - Dinosaurs and Jeff Goldblum in his prime? I mean, c'mon!
  • Terminator 2: Judgement Day - Undisputed God Emperor of action movies.
  • Predator - There's no end to the one-liners.
  • Alien - While I'm not the biggest horror fan, it's hard to ignore the heavy weight champions.
